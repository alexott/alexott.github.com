Introduction

Currently we cannot pick out a content filtering into the dedicated branch of information security, so it mixed with other branches. For the information security, content filtering is very important, as it allow to detect an potentially dangerous things, and process them correctly. Approaches, that appears during development of products for content filtering, currently used for intrusion detection (IDS), detecting malicious code, and other negative actions.

On the base of new technologies and products for content filtering, are created an additional services for users, increased a quality of protection, and allowed not to only react to the existed threats, and to prevent a new, not known threats.[1] [2]
