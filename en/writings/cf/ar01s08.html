Peer-to-peer filtering

Usage of peer-to-peer (p2p) networks make them very dangerous for organizations due following possible threats:

  1. propagation of malicious code;

  2. information leakage;

  3. distribution of copyrighted data could lead to legal abuse of company;

  4. increasing load of data channels.

Exists big number of networks, that use peer-to-peer for data transfer. Some of them use schemes with central servers, that coordinate users, but some of them are fully distributed, without one coordinators. Second case— is worst for filtering software manufacturers. For controlling and processing of p2p-traffic exists following solutions:

  1. SurfControl Instant Messaging Filter, that process p2p together with instant messaging;

  2. Websense Enterprise also provide detection and blocking of peer-to-peer traffic;

  3. Webwasher Instant Message Filter allow to control user's access to different peer-to-peer networks.

Usage of these, or other products could significantly decrease risks from usage of peer-to-peer networks.
