I've been working with functional programming languages for a long time. Lisp was first functional language, that I studied. Now I mostly program in Clojure, but also play with Erlang, OCaml, Haskell, Scheme and Common Lisp.

I'm author of many articles, including articles on functional programming languages (many of them in Russian, including, published in the Russian magazine called "Practice of functional programming". I also translated to English my review of existing literature on functional programming that was published in the first issue of this magazine.
